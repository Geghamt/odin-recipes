Meatloaf

The best classic Meatloaf

This amazing homemade meatloaf recipe is tender

and juicy on the inside with a sweet and tangy topping.

  1. Add 90% lean ground beef to a large bowl
  2. Add dried bread crumbs and some diced onion
  3. Add milk, an egg, ketchup, and worcestershire sauce
  4. Add dried parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper
